Blender Siggraph 2014 Demoreel Call for Content

SIGGRAPH 2014 is almost here! As usualy, we will be putting together a demoreel of the best animations to come out of the Blender community. The reel will be shown at our Siggraph booth in August, and later released online for public viewing. If you have work to share, or know a project that deserves the attention, please help us make the reel great!

To get started, take a look at last year's demoreel:

General Criteria

  • Accepted formats are: Stills with breakdowns, Finished Animations, Breakdowns, Timelapse, Game Engine captures
  • All content must be at least 1920x1080 or higher in resolution with minimal compression artifact
  • All content must involved Blender in some way during its production
  • Submission deadline: July 1s, 2014
